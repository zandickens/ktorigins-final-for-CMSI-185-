Where the Horror Began!


K'tah! Origins (Project Name: K'torigins) takes us back to the beginning of the outbreak... before our heroes met, before the giant boulder became sentient, and before a 3rd dimension was added to reality. In K'torigins, you play as one of our isolated heroes scrambling to survive the onslought of the zombie horde... in 2D!
